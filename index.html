About

Delicate tattoos with a calm, considered approach.

Sa.li Tattoo creates elegant fine line work for clients who want something understated, feminine, and deeply personal. Every piece is designed to feel light on the skin while still carrying intention and presence.

Refined artistry, personal detail

From soft florals to minimal symbolic work, each tattoo is approached with a clean eye for composition, flow, and longevity. The experience is thoughtful from consultation to aftercare, with a focus on making clients feel comfortable and beautifully looked after.

  • Specialising in fine line, delicate ornament, botanical and micro-detail tattoos.

FAQ

Everything you may want to know before your appointment

These answers set a calm, polished tone while leaving room to personalise details later.

How should I prepare for my tattoo appointment?

Arrive well rested, hydrated, and having eaten beforehand. Avoid alcohol before your appointment and wear something comfortable that makes the tattoo area easy to access.

How does healing work for fine line tattoos?

Healing is usually straightforward with the right aftercare. Keep the area clean, avoid soaking or friction, and follow the aftercare guidance shared during your appointment.

How is pricing decided?

Pricing depends on size, placement, complexity, and session time. The best way to get an accurate quote is to send your idea and references through Instagram.

Do you offer custom designs?

Yes. Custom work is welcome, especially for delicate concepts that need thoughtful composition and placement. Reference imagery is helpful, but each design is refined to suit you.
